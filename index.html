OVERVIEW

Muthayammal Engineering College,an Autonomous Institution Affiliated to Anna University,Chennai, was established in the year 2000 under the aegis of Muthayammal Educational Trust and Research Foundation with the motto to uplift the rural community through Technical Education. The College has grown as a reputed institution in the past 20 years and progressing with the aim to become Center of Excellence in Engineering Education, Research and Development.
